in   -   en

Carlos is in the house.   -   Carlos está en la casa.

The computer is in the car.   -   El ordenador / la computadora está en el coche / el carro.

to do   -   hacer

I want to do it here.   -   Quiero hacerlo aquí.

to know   -   saber

I want to know.   -   Quiero saber.

I know   -   (yo) sé

I know where the chair is.   -   (Yo) sé dónde está la silla.

you know   -   (tú) sabes

Do you know that I do it today? / Do you know that I am doing it today?   -   ¿Sabes que lo hago hoy?

Do you know when you have to leave?   -   ¿Sabes cuándo tienes que salir?

he, she   -   él, ella

she knows   -   (ella) sabe

He knows why.   -   (Él) sabe por qué.

He does it.   -   (Él) lo hace.

to eat   -   comer

I eat   -   como

you eat   -   comes

Are you eating?   -   ¿comes?

She eats on a chair   -   (Ella) come en una silla.

to have dinner   -   cenar

I want to have dinner there.   -   Quiero cenar allí.

you have dinner   -   cenas

When do you have dinner?   -   ¿Cuándo cenas?

he has dinner. / he is having dinner.   -   cena

Where does he have dinner?   -   ¿Dónde cena?
